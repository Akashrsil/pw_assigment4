Q2: What are HTML Entities ? LIst out 5 commonly used HTML entities?
Ans: HTML entities are special codes used to represent characters that have special meaning in HTML or characters that cannot be easily typed on a keyboard. They are used to ensure that these characters are displayed correctly in web browsers and to avoid parsing errors.
Commonly used HTML Entities
&lt; - Less-than sign (<)
&gt; - Greater-than sign (>)
&amp; - Ampersand (&)
&quot; - Double quotation mark (")
&apos; - Apostrophe or single quotation mark (')
